Virtual Labs

Virtual Labs is an initiative by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, India under the National Mission on Education through ICT. Virtual Labs are interactive simulators with a user friendly interface that mimic physical laboratories. The student can perform the experiment in a virtual environment. Virtual labs act as a supplement to students and instructors for better understanding of the subject and help overcome the lack of resources. An engaging, self explanatory user interface must be created so as to make learning more exciting and interesting for the student.

Power Electronics

Power Electronics is the study of switching electronic circuits in order to control the flow of electrical energy. Power Electronics is the technology behind switching power supplies, power converters, power inverters, motor drives, and motor soft starters.

Power Electronics involves the study of

  • Power semiconductor devices - their physics, characteristics, drive requirements and their protection for optimum utilisation of their capacities,
  • Power semiconductor devices - their physics, characteristics, drive requirements and their protection for optimum utilisation of their capacities,
  • Control strategies of the converters,
  • Digital, analogue and microelectronics involved,
  • Capacitive and magnetic energy storage elements,
  • Rotating and static electrical devices,
  • Quality of waveforms generated,
  • Electro Magnetic and Radio Frequency Interference,
  • Thermal Management
